How to Find Us

PuzzleStay

PuzzleStay is an innovative web application designed to revolutionize the hospitality industry by integrating interactive escape room-style experiences into hotel stays.

The app connects travelers with hotels offering immersive puzzle-based accommodations, where guests solve challenges to unlock exclusive experiences, discounts, or amenities.

How to Play

Our games are for young families who would like to get busy and have fun during their stays.
